working processes

How it works

step 1

list your charger

Are you the proud owner of an EV charger? Simply provide details about its location, availability and any specific instructions, making it visible to nearby EV drivers in need.

step 2

find chargers

Are you running low on battery? Use our app to locate nearby chargers available for sharing. Filter by location and other criteria to find the perfect match for your needs.

step 3

charge and earn

Once you've found a charger, plug in your EV and enjoy a hassle-free charging experience. As a charger owner, you earn for every session hosted on your charger.

Why should I become a host?

Are you the proud owner of an EV charger at home? If so, you likely understand that it's not in constant use. Why not put it to work and earn rewards in the process? By becoming a Co Charger Host with Spark Share, you have the opportunity to not only monetize your charger but also play a crucial role in supporting your neighbors' transition to electric vehicles.

Charge your EV

How does it work?

Use the Spark Share app to find nearby EV chargers.
Once you've found a charger, simply plug in your EV.
Keep track of your charging session through the app.
Payment for the charging session is handled through the Spark Share app.
Once you've reached your desired charge level, safely disconnect the charging cable from your vehicle.
Share your experience by leaving a review on the app, this helps other EV drivers make informed decisions about where to charge.
